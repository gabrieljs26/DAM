Quick hummus recipe

this recipe makes quick , tasty hummus , with no messing.It has been adapted form a number of different recipes that i had read over the years.

Hummus is a delicious thick pasted used heavily in greek and middle eastern dishes. It is very taty with salad , grilled meats and pitta beads.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400gr) of chick peas (garbanzo beans)
  • 175g of tahini
  • 6 sundried tomatoes
  • half of red pepper
  • a pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • a dash of olive oil

Instructions

  • remove the skin form the garlic , and chop coarsely
  • remove all the seeds and stalk form the pepper , and cop coarsely
  • add all the ingredients into a food processor
  • process all the ingredients into a paste
  • if you want a coarse "chunky" hummus , process it for a short time
  • if you want a smooth hummus , process it for a loger time

for a different flavour , you could try blending in a small measure of lemon and coriander , chili pepper , lime and chipotle , harissa and mint , or spinach and feta cheese. Experiment and see what works for you

storage

refrigerate the finished hummus in a sealed container. You should be able to use it for about a week after you have made it . If it starts to become fizzy , you should defnitely discard it.

Hummus is suitable for frezzing; you should thaw it and use it within a couple of months.
